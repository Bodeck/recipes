Ingredients:
* 500g strong wheat flour
* 325 ml of luke worm water
* 7 g of dry yeast (1 sashet)
* 5 table-spoons of extra virgin olive oil
* 1 tea-spoon of salt
Procedure:
1) Disolve yeast in water and put aside for 5 minutes
2) Mix salt with salt in large bowl
3) Mix wheat with 1 table spoon of olive oil and flour.
4) Once ingredients are combine, put dough on table dusted with flour and start knee. It should take about 8 minutes.
5) Put dough back in to bowl dusted with flour. Cover with wet kitchen tovel and set aside for proofing for 45 minutes.
